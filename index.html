Welcome to JMCV.

Basic code and samples for bring together jMonkeyEngine and JavaCV. Use case scenarios include 3D game enhancement, AR, etc.

Sample code.

jMonkeyEngine 3.0 project jmcv1.0, that you will find in this repo, demonstrates how things works. Just run it as a jMonkeyEngine 3.0 project. The demo is mygame.Main.class. It is an AR environment composed by a 3D char having your webcam dynamic captured video as your scenario. If you move your face to the left of a imaginary vertical line on the center of the screen, the char will dance. If you move it to the right, it will remain idle.
